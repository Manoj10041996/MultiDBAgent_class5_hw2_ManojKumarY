# Shipping Policy — StockPulse

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026  
**Last updated:** 1 April 2026

---

## Overview

StockPulse ships products to customers across the United States and internationally. This policy outlines shipping options, estimated delivery times, and how we handle delays and lost packages.

---

## Domestic Shipping (United States)

StockPulse offers three domestic shipping tiers:

| Shipping Option | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|----------------|--------------------|------|
| Standard Ground | 5–7 business days | Free on orders over $50; $4.99 otherwise |
| Expedited | 2–3 business days | $9.99 flat rate |
| Overnight Express | Next business day (order by 2 PM EST) | $24.99 flat rate |

- Orders placed before **2:00 PM Eastern Time** on business days are processed the same day.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.
- Free standard shipping is automatically applied at checkout for qualifying orders.

---

## International Shipping

StockPulse ships to **over 40 countries** worldwide. International shipping is handled via our logistics partners (DHL Express and FedEx International).

| Region | Estimated Delivery | Starting Cost |
|--------|--------------------|---------------|
| Canada & Mexico | 3–5 business days | $14.99 |
| Europe | 7–14 business days | $19.99 |
| Asia-Pacific | 10–21 business days | $24.99 |
| Rest of World | 14–28 business days | $29.99 |

**Important international notes:**

- International customers are responsible for all **customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees** imposed by the destination country.
- StockPulse does not ship to countries under trade embargo or export control restrictions.
- Certain products (e.g., lithium battery devices) may have restricted shipping options to some countries due to carrier regulations.
- International orders are **not eligible for same-day processing**.

---

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a **shipping confirmation email** with a tracking number. Track your package via:

- The StockPulse customer portal (My Orders section).
- Directly on the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, DHL, or USPS depending on the shipping method selected).

Tracking information may take up to **4 hours** to become active after the shipment confirmation email.

---

## Shipping Delays

StockPulse makes every effort to meet the estimated delivery dates. However, the following circumstances may cause delays:

- **Weather events**: Severe weather affecting carrier operations.
- **High-demand periods**: Peak seasons (November–December) may add 1–3 business days.
- **Address issues**: Incomplete or incorrect shipping addresses will cause delays. StockPulse will contact the customer within 1 business day if an address issue is detected.
- **Customs clearance** (international): Delays in customs are outside StockPulse's control.

If your order has not arrived within **3 business days** past the estimated delivery date, please open a support ticket for investigation.

---

## Lost or Stolen Packages

If a package shows as **delivered** but was not received:

1. Check with neighbours and building management first.
2. Wait **24 hours** as carrier updates can sometimes be premature.
3. Open a support ticket within **72 hours** of the marked delivery date.

StockPulse will initiate a carrier investigation. If the package is confirmed lost, a replacement will be shipped or a full refund issued within **7 business days**.

StockPulse is **not responsible** for packages confirmed delivered to the correct address and subsequently stolen. We recommend adding **package insurance** (available at checkout for $1.99) for high-value orders.

---

## P.O. Box and Military APO/FPO Addresses

StockPulse ships to P.O. Box addresses via USPS for standard domestic shipments. APO/FPO military addresses are supported using USPS Priority Mail.

Expedited and overnight services are **not available** for P.O. Box or APO/FPO addresses.
