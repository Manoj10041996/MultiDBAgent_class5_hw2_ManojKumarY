# Discount & Promotions Policy — StockPulse

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026  
**Last updated:** 1 April 2026

---

## Overview

StockPulse offers a range of discount programmes and promotional pricing to reward loyal customers and incentivise bulk purchases. This policy defines eligibility, calculation rules, and stacking restrictions for all discount types.

---

## Bulk Order Discounts

Customers purchasing large quantities of a single SKU in one transaction are eligible for automatic bulk discounts:

| Quantity Ordered (per SKU) | Discount Applied |
|---------------------------|-----------------|
| 5–9 units | 5% off unit price |
| 10–24 units | 10% off unit price |
| 25–49 units | 15% off unit price |
| 50+ units | 20% off unit price |

**Conditions:**

- Bulk discounts apply **per SKU per order**, not across multiple SKUs combined.
- The discount is applied automatically at checkout — no coupon code required.
- Bulk discounts are available to both individual and business accounts.
- Bulk discounts do **not** apply to products already marked **On Sale** or **Clearance**.
- Orders qualifying for bulk discounts must ship to a **single address**.

---

## Loyalty Programme — StockPulse Rewards

StockPulse Rewards members earn points on every eligible purchase:

| Account Tier | Points Earned per $1 Spent | Additional Benefit |
|-------------|---------------------------|-------------------|
| Bronze (0–499 points) | 1 point | None |
| Silver (500–1,999 points) | 1.5 points | Free expedited shipping on orders over $30 |
| Gold (2,000+ points) | 2 points | Free expedited shipping on all orders + priority support |

**Redemption rules:**

- 100 points = $1 in store credit.
- Points can be redeemed against any order, including sale items.
- Points expire **12 months** after the last purchase.
- Points are **not earned** on gift card purchases, shipping fees, or taxes.
- Points adjustments for returns: returned items' points are deducted from the account at the time the refund is processed.

## Seasonal Promotions

StockPulse runs seasonal sales events throughout the year:

| Event | Typical Dates | Discount Type |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| Spring Sale | March 15–31 | Category-wide 10–20% off |
| Back to School | August 1–31 | Electronics 15% off |
| Black Friday | Last Friday of November | Site-wide 20–40% off |
| Cyber Monday | Following Monday after Black Friday | Electronics + Accessories 25–35% off |
| Holiday Sale | December 10–24 | Site-wide 15% off |

- Seasonal promotions are applied automatically at checkout.
- Seasonal discounts **cannot be combined** with other promotional codes or coupon codes.
- Seasonal promotions apply to **in-stock items only** — backordered items are excluded.

---

## Coupon Codes

Promotional coupon codes are distributed via email, social media, and affiliate partners.

**Coupon stacking rules:**

- **Only one coupon code** may be applied per order.
- Coupons cannot be combined with seasonal promotional pricing.
- Coupons **can be combined** with loyalty points redemption.
- Coupons **cannot** be applied retroactively to already-placed orders.
- Coupons are valid for the date range specified on the coupon only.

---

## Employee and Partner Discounts

Verified StockPulse employees and registered business partners receive:

- Employees: **30% off** all items (excluding clearance, already-discounted, and bulk-order-tier items).
- Business Partners: **15% off** all items, combinable with bulk discounts.

Employee and partner discounts require account verification and are applied at the account level, not via coupon codes.

---

## Price Match Guarantee

StockPulse will match the price of any identical product sold by an authorised reseller if:

1. The competitor's price is verifiable and currently live.
2. The product is identical (same model, SKU, and configuration).
3. The request is made within **7 days** of purchase.

Price match requests do not apply to marketplace sellers, auction sites, or liquidation sellers.
