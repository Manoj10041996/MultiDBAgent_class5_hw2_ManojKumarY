# Return & Refund Policy — StockPulse

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026  
**Last updated:** 1 April 2026

---

## Overview

StockPulse is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy describes the conditions under which customers may return products, how refunds are processed, and the timelines involved.

---

## Eligibility

Customers may return most products within **30 days of the delivery date**, provided the item is in its original, unused condition with all original packaging and accessories included.

The following conditions make a return eligible:

- The item arrived damaged or defective.
- The wrong item was delivered.
- The item does not match the product description on the website.
- The customer changed their mind (restocking fee of 10% applies for change-of-mind returns).

The following items are **not eligible for return**:

- Digital download products or software licences once activated.
- Perishable goods.
- Personalised or custom-made items.
- Items marked **Final Sale** at the time of purchase.
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery.

---

## Damaged Goods

Customers who receive a damaged or defective item must:

1. Report the damage within **48 hours** of delivery by opening a support ticket.
2. Provide **photographic or video evidence** of the damage at the time of the return request.
3. Keep the original packaging until the return is resolved.

Damaged goods may be returned within **30 days of delivery**. StockPulse will cover the return shipping cost for verified damaged or defective items.

Refunds for damaged goods are processed within **5–7 business days** after the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse.

---

## Return Process

1. **Initiate a return request** via the StockPulse customer portal or by contacting support.
2. Receive a **Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA)** number within 1 business day.
3. Ship the item using the provided prepaid label (for eligible defective/wrong-item returns) or at your own cost (for change-of-mind returns).
4. Receive a refund or exchange confirmation once the item is inspected.

---

## Refund Timelines

| Reason | Refund Timeline |
|--------|----------------|
| Damaged / defective item | 5–7 business days after warehouse receipt |
| Wrong item delivered | 3–5 business days after warehouse receipt |
| Change of mind (10% restocking fee) | 7–10 business days after warehouse receipt |
| Item not as described | 5–7 business days after warehouse receipt |

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** only. Store credit may be offered as an alternative at the customer's discretion.

---

## Exchanges

Customers requesting an exchange instead of a refund will receive priority processing. The replacement item will be shipped within **2 business days** of the returned item being received and inspected.

---

## Non-Returnable Items (Extended List)

- Gift cards and store credit vouchers.
- Opened consumables (ink cartridges, batteries, cleaning solutions).
- Items with tampered, removed, or damaged serial-number stickers.
- Items not purchased directly from StockPulse (third-party marketplace orders).

---

## Contact

For all return and refund queries, open a support ticket at **support.stockpulse.com** or email **returns@stockpulse.com**.
